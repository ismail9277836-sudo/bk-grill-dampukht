Grill Speciality

The Art of Charcoal Fire

Our kebabs are marinated overnight in a blend of fresh herbs, yoghurt, and hand-ground spices — then cooked over live charcoal that gives each skewer its signature smokiness.

From chapli kabab to seekh, boti, and full lamb tikka — every piece is tended until perfectly charred on the outside and irresistibly juicy within.

Ancient Technique
Dampukht

Sealed in Flavour, Slow & Low

Dampukht is the crown jewel of our kitchen — a Mughal technique over 500 years old. Meat is sealed inside a pot with dough, then slow-cooked over gentle heat until every fibre absorbs the richness of whole spices.

The result is fall-off-the-bone tenderness with layers of aroma that no other cooking method can replicate.
